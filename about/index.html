About us

Founded in 2017, Cay's Styling and Maintenance emerged from a simple yet powerful concept: to help individuals discover their unique style identity while providing unparalleled service and expertise. What started as assisting adults in choosing clothing pieces in exchange for a single item blossomed into something much more significant.

With a knack for style and an innate ability to understand the intricacies of personal expression, our founder quickly garnered acclaim for their talent. Initially, it was friends seeking advice for date outfits that ignited the flame of Cay's reputation for excellence. The resounding reviews propelled Cay's into the realm of wedding styling, where groomsmen were adorned with sophistication and elegance under our guidance.

But Cay's Styling and Maintenance is not limited by gender or occasion. We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and empowered by their appearance, regardless of age, gender, or background. Our inclusive approach ensures that every individual receives personalized attention and tailored recommendations to suit their unique lifestyle and preferences. Growing up in a Congolese environment filled with the flamboyance and flair of sapeurs, our founder was deeply influenced by the ethos of style as a form of self-expression. This upbringing instilled in them a passion for fashion and an appreciation for the transformative power of clothing. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion, revamping your wardrobe, or simply seeking guidance on refining your personal style, Cay's Styling and Maintenance is here to accompany you on your journey towards sartorial excellence.
